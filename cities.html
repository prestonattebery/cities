BlockCities consists of 3,232 unique architecture NFTs.

BlockCities was inspired by CryptoKitties and the erc721 token standarded invented in late 2017

Each building was meticulously designed over the course of two years from 2018 to 2020.

BlockCities is designed in the classic isometic illustration style pioneered by early 2.5d games in the 90s, including SimCity.

BlockCities is an erc721 NFT contract on Ethereum.
It was deployed on April 2, 2019.

The BlockCities Genesis Building was minted on the same day and sold on May 16, 2019 for 7.2 ETH

BlockCities was the first NFT project to introduce dynamic pricing.

The BlockCities Mystery Machine sold buildings at 0.05 ETH and increased in value by 0.0003 ETH every time an NFT was minted.
The price descreased by 0.0003 ETH every 50 blocks a new NFT was not minted.

BlockCities includes 2665 generative buildings, 334 Classics, and 233 Specials.

Generatives are determined by on-chain random number generation. Each is unique.
They are mashups of real world buildings with unique colors, roofs, cores, and ground floors.

Classics are clones of real world buildings from New York, Atlanta, Chicago, and San Francisco.

Specials are custom designed buildings.

BlockCities started as collectibles, became a game, and now are just collectibles.

BlockCities' last building was minted on May 6, 2020 by foundersinc.eth.

BlockCities buildings can be purchased on Opensea.

$CITIES is a BlockCities index fund live on NFTX.

$CITIES can be minted by depositing 1x BlockCities NFT into the fund.

$CITIES can be redeemed for 1x random building from the fund.

$CITIES is currently not for sale
