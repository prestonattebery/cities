BlockCities consists of 3,232 unique architecture NFTs.

BlockCities' NFT contract was deployed on April 2, 2019.

BlockCities' Genesis Building was minted on the same day and sold on May 16, 2019 for 7.2 ETH

BlockCities includes 2665 generative buildings determined by on-chain random number generation.

BlockCities Generatives are mashups of real world buildings with unique colors, roofs, cores, and ground floors.

BlockCities includes 233 Specials.

BlockCities Specials are custom designed buildings including 1 Brooklyn Gold and 83 Founder Buildings

BlockCities includes 334 Classics.

BlockCities Classics are clones of real world buildings from New York, Atlanta, Chicago, San Francisco, Japan, Paris, London, Stuttgart, and Dessau.

BlockCities started as collectibles, became a game, and now are just collectibles.

BlockCities' last building was minted on May 6, 2020 by foundersinc.eth. Respect.

BlockCities buildings can be purchased on Opensea.

$CITIES is a BlockCities index fund live on NFTX.

$CITIES can be minted by depositing 1 BlockCities NFT into the fund.

$CITIES can be redeemed for one random building from the index fund at anytime.
