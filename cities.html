BlockCities consists of 3,232 unique architecture NFTs.

The BlockCities NFT contract was deployed on April 2, 2019.

The BlockCities Genesis Building was minted on the same day and sold on May 16, 2019 for 7.2 ETH

2665 are generative.
Mashups of real world buildings with unique colors, roofs, cores, and ground floors
They were determined by on-chain random number generation.
Some are really tall. Some are really tiny.

233 are Specials.
Custom designed buildings
Including a 1 of 1 Brooklyn Gold
83 Founder Buildings given to every builder who minted before 500 total buildings.

334 are Classics.
Clones of real world buildings from New York, Atlanta, Chicago, San Francisco, Japan, Paris, London, Stuttgart, and Dessau.

BlockCities started as collectibles, became a game, and now are just collectibles.

The last BlockCities NFT was minted on May 6, 2020 by foundersinc.eth. Respect.

BlockCities NFTs can be purchased on Opensea.

The BlockCities index fund is live on NFTX.

Anyone can deposit 1 BlockCities NFT in exchange for 1 $CITIES token

$CITIES can be redeemed for one random building from the index fund at anytime.

$CITIES is currently not for sale
